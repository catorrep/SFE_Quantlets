Quantlet:     var_pot
---------------------------------------------------------------------
Description:  var_pot provides Value at Risk estimates computed with
              Peaks Over Treshold model with generalized Pareto
              distribution.
---------------------------------------------------------------------
Usage:        [var,ksi,beta,u]=var_pot(y,h,p,q);
---------------------------------------------------------------------
Inputs:       y - vector of returns
              p - quantile for at which Value at Risk should be estimated
              h - size of the window
              q - scalar, e.g. 0.1
---------------------------------------------------------------------
Output:       ksi, beta - parameters from POT model
              var - VaR estimates at 0.95 quantile
